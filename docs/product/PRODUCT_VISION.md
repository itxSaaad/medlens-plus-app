# Product Vision: MedLens+

## One-line Positioning

MedLens+ turns fragmented medical reports into a clear, safe, longitudinal health narrative for patients and doctors.

## Target Users

- Adults managing chronic conditions
- Caregivers managing family member history
- Physicians needing faster consultation prep

## Core Jobs-To-Be-Done

- "Help me understand what changed since my last report."
- "Show me which values are abnormal and why they matter."
- "Prepare me for my doctor visit with focused questions."

## Product Differentiators

- Multi-report timeline and trends
- Source-grounded interpretation
- Missing-data detection
- Doctor-ready brief, not generic chatbot output

## Guardrails

- No diagnosis claims
- No medication prescription advice
- Encourage professional consultation
