- Status: BACKLOG

## Problem

## Scope
- Generate concise follow-up questions from detected trends
- Prioritize by potential clinical significance

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Questions are linked to report evidence.
2. Duplicate/redundant questions are removed.
3. Output tone is patient-friendly and concise.
