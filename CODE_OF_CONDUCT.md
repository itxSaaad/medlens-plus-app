# Code of Conduct

We are committed to an open, respectful, and inclusive community.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Focus on product quality and user safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or abuse.
- Sharing sensitive medical/personal data.
- Malicious contributions.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban users violating this policy.

## Reporting
Open a private security/community report via `SECURITY.md` contacts.
